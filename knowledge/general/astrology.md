# Astrology (Zodiac Signs)

## Western Zodiac
- **Aries** (Mar 21-Apr 19): fire, cardinal, ruled by Mars. Traits: energetic, competitive, impulsive, courageous, direct, short-tempered. Element: fire. Quality: cardinal (initiating). Matches with Leo, Sagittarius. Compatible: fire signs. Opposites with Libra
- **Taurus** (Apr 20-May 20): earth, fixed, ruled by Venus. Traits: stubborn, reliable, sensual, patient, practical, loves comfort + routine. Element: earth. Quality: fixed. Matches Virgo, Capricorn, Cancer, Pisces. With Venus = aesthetic sensibility, love of possessions, money drive. Opposites: Scorpio
- **Gemini** (May 21-Jun 20): air, mutable, ruled by Mercury. Traits: adaptable, curious, chatty, witty, restless, dual-natured, loves learning. Element: air. Quality: mutable (flexible, communicative). Matches Libra, Aquarius. "The twins" — twin archetype, dual personality, versatility. Opposites: Sagittarius
- **Cancer** (Jun 21-Jul 22): water, cardinal, ruled by Moon. Traits: emotional, nurturing, protective, moody, home-loving, intuitive. Element: water. Quality: cardinal. Matches Scorpio, Pisces, Taurus, Virgo. Moon = emotions, mother/nurture, domestic sphere. Opposites: Capricorn
- **Leo** (Jul 23-Aug 22): fire, fixed, ruled by Sun. Traits: confident, generous, dramatic, theatrical, warm-hearted, attention-seeking, loyal. Element: fire. Quality: fixed. Matches Aries, Sagittarius, Libra, Gemini. Ruling Sun = central/sun/ego, individuality, drama. Opposites: Aquarius
- **Virgo** (Aug 23-Sep 22): earth, mutable, ruled by Mercury. Traits: analytical, detail-oriented, practical, perfectionist, modest, health-conscious. Element: earth. Quality: mutable. Matches Taurus, Capricorn, Cancer, Scorpio. Mercury in Virgo = analytical mind, criticism, service to others. Opposites: Pisces
- **Libra** (Sep 23-Oct 22): air, cardinal, ruled by Venus. Traits: diplomatic, charming, fair-minded, indecisive, social, hates conflict, loves beauty. Element: air. Quality: cardinal. Matches Gemini, Aquarius, Leo, Sagittarius. Ruling Venus = relationships, love, aesthetics. Opposites: Aries
- **Scorpio** (Oct 23-Nov 21): water, fixed, ruled by Pluto (modern) / Mars (traditional). Traits: intense, passionate, secretive, determined, mysterious, resourceful, magnetic. Element: water. Quality: fixed. Matches Cancer, Pisces, Virgo, Capricorn. Ruling Pluto = death/rebirth, transformation, hidden realms. Opposites: Taurus
- **Sagittarius** (Nov 22-Dec 21): fire, mutable, ruled by Jupiter. Traits: adventurous, optimistic, blunt, freedom-loving, philosophical, restless. Element: fire. Quality: mutable. Matches Aries, Leo, Libra, Aquarius. Ruling Jupiter = expansion, luck, higher learning, long-distance travel. Opposites: Gemini
- **Capricorn** (Dec 22-Jan 19): earth, cardinal, ruled by Saturn. Traits: ambitious, disciplined, responsible, cautious, reserved, authoritative. Element: earth. Quality: cardinal. Matches Taurus, Virgo, Scorpio, Pisces. Ruling Saturn = discipline, restriction, responsibility, time. Opposites: Cancer
- **Aquarius** (Jan 20-Feb 18): air, fixed, ruled by Uranus (modern) / Saturn (traditional). Traits: innovative, humanitarian, rebellious, detached, intellectual, unconventional. Element: air. Quality: fixed. Matches Gemini, Libra, Aries, Sagittarius. Ruling Uranus = revolution, eccentricity, sudden change. Opposites: Leo
- **Pisces** (Feb 19-Mar 20): water, mutable, ruled by Neptune (modern) / Jupiter (traditional). Traits: compassionate, artistic, intuitive, escapist, sensitive, dreamy, psychic. Element: water. Quality: mutable. Matches Cancer, Scorpio, Taurus, Capricorn. Ruling Neptune = dreams, illusion, spirituality, escapism. Opposites: Virgo

## Planets in Astrology
- **Sun**: ego, identity, core self, vitality — your sign. What sign was Sun in at your birth = your "zodiac sign"
- **Moon**: emotions, instincts, subconscious, mother/nurture. How you process feelings
- **Mercury**: communication, thinking style, intellect. If Mercury is retrograde at birth = you process things differently
- **Venus**: love, beauty, art, relationships, what attracts you, values
- **Mars**: action, drive, aggression, sexual desire, how you pursue goals
- **Jupiter**: expansion, luck, abundance, higher learning, growth, optimism
- **Saturn**: discipline, restrictions, challenges, responsibility, life lessons, career
- **Uranus**: change, revolution, uniqueness, rebellion, unexpected events, eccentricity
- **Neptune**: dreams, spirituality, intuition, illusion, confusion, addiction, artistic inspiration
- **Pluto**: transformation, power, rebirth, control, deep psychological processes (generational planet; modern)
- **Chiron**: the wounded healer — deepest wounds + ability to heal others

## Houses & Rising Sign
- **Rising sign/ Ascendant**: the sign on eastern horizon at birth. Changes every ~2 hours. How you appear to others, first impression, mask, persona. Rising sign determines houses
- **Houses (12)**: 1st (self, appearance), 2nd (money, values), 3rd (communication, siblings, short trips), 4th (home, family, roots), 5th (creativity, children, romance, fun), 6th (health, daily routine, work/service), 7th (partnership, marriage, open enemies), 8th (shared resources, sexuality, death/ rebirth, transformation, other people's money), 9th (philosophy, travel, higher education, religion), 10th (career, public image, achievements), 11th (friends, groups, hopes, networks), 12th (subconscious, secrets, hidden things, solitude, institutions)
- **Natal chart**: birth snapshot (positions of all planets + signs + houses). Birth details: date, time, location needed for accurate chart. Free tools: astro.com, cafeastrology.com, timepassages. Each planet in sign + house = personality aspects. Aspects: angles between planets (conjunction 0°, trine 120°, sextile 60°, square 90°, opposition 180°). Hard aspects (square, opposition) = tension, growth. Soft aspects (trine, sextile) = ease, talent
  - Big three: Sun (ego/identity), Moon (emotions), Rising (persona). What people usually mean when they ask "what's your sign?" — their Sun sign. Actually full chart is Sun + Moon + Rising + all planets
- **Retrograde**: planet appears to move backward from Earth perspective. Mercury retrograde: communication glitches, travel delays, tech issues — 3-4x/year, 3 weeks each. Not scientifically proven but widely believed in astrology: avoid signing contracts, buying electronics, starting major projects. Venus retrograde: relationships reviewed. Mars retrograde: forward momentum slowed in action areas. Saturn retrograde: revisiting responsibilities. Planets are always retrograde from Earth's perspective when they pass us on a faster orbit (faster = outer planets retrograde yearly)
  - Solar Return: chart for the moment the Sun returns to your birth sign — annual birthday chart predicting themes of that year
