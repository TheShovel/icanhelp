# Geography Deep Dive: Major Countries

## United States
- **Geography**: 3rd in area (9.83M km²). 50 states, diverse biomes (tundra Alaska to tropical Hawaii/ Florida, deserts Southwest, Great Plains, Appalachians, Rocky Mountains)
  - Time zones: Eastern, Central, Mountain, Pacific, Alaska, Hawaii-Aleutian
  - Major rivers: Mississippi-Missouri (longest system), Colorado, Rio Grande, Columbia, Yukon
  - Great Lakes: Superior, Michigan, Huron, Erie, Ontario (21% of world's fresh surface water)
- **Government**: federal republic, constitutional (1787). 3 branches (Congress, President, Supreme Court). Political parties: Democratic (center-left), Republican (center-right)
- **Economy**: largest economy ($27T GDP, 2024). Top sectors: services (80%), manufacturing (10%), agriculture (1% but world's top food exporter)
  - Leading companies: Apple, Microsoft, Amazon, Google (Alphabet), Meta, Tesla, Berkshire Hathaway, JPMorgan Chase, Exxon, Johnson & Johnson
- **Demographics**: 341M (3rd largest). Highly diverse — multi-ethnic, born from immigration. Official language: none (English de facto). Life expectancy: 77 years (low for developed)
- **Cultural influence**: Hollywood (film/TV), Silicon Valley (tech), Wall Street (finance), music (jazz, blues, rock, hip-hop, country), fast food, brands (Coca-Cola, McDonald's, Levi's)

## China
- **Geography**: 4th largest (9.6M km²). Himalayas (southwest), Gobi Desert (north), Yangtze and Yellow Rivers (central/east), tropical south, temperate east, arid west
  - Time zone: one (UTC+8) for entire country (geographically spans 5 zones)
  - Great Wall (21,000 km), Yangtze (6,300 km — 3rd longest river), Three Gorges Dam (largest hydro plant)
- **Government**: single-party communist (CCP), authoritarian. Xi Jinping (paramount leader since 2012). National People's Congress (rubber-stamp legislature)
  - Hong Kong: "one country, two systems" since 1997 — separate legal system, freedoms eroding (national security law 2020)
- **Economy**: 2nd largest ($17.7T GDP, PPP = ~$33T, largest in PPP). Fastest major economic growth in history (10%+ annually 1980s-2010s)
  - Leading sectors: manufacturing (30% of global output), technology (Huawei, Tencent/WeChat, Alibaba, TikTok/ByteDance), infrastructure (Belt and Road Initiative), electric vehicles (BYD)
  - Belt and Road: infrastructure/loans to 150+ countries — economic influence, debt concerns, strategic expansion
- **Demographics**: 1.4B (2nd, about to decline — projected 800M by 2100). 56 ethnic groups (Han = 92%). One-child policy 1979-2015 → aging crisis (median age 38, rising fast)
  - Urbanization: 65% urban (vs 20% in 1980). Megacities: Shanghai (27M), Beijing (22M), Guangzhou (19M), Shenzhen (18M), Chengdu (16M)
- **Issues**: pollution (air, water, soil — improving but still severe), political repression (Uighurs/Xinjiang, Tibet, Hong Kong), human rights, censorship (Great Firewall blocks western internet), Taiwan (claimed but self-governing)

## India
- **Geography**: 7th largest (3.3M km²). Himalayas (north), Indo-Gangetic plain (most fertile/dense), Deccan Plateau (central/south), Thar Desert (west), tropical coastline (7,500 km)
  - Rivers: Ganges (most sacred, most polluted), Brahmaputra, Indus (mostly in Pakistan), Godavari
  - Climate: tropical monsoon — monsoon (June-September) critical for agriculture, floods also common
- **Government**: world's largest democracy, federal republic, 28 states + 8 union territories. Prime Minister (Narendra Modi, since 2014). Parliamentary system
  - Secular constitution — major parties: BJP (Hindu-nationalist right), Congress (center-left)
- **Economy**: 5th largest ($3.9T GDP, PPP ~$14T — 3rd). Fastest-growing major economy (6-7%/year)
  - IT/services (Bangalore, Hyderabad — global tech hub), pharma (generic drugs), manufacturing, agriculture (17% of GDP, 50% of employment)
  - Space: ISRO (Indian Space Research Org) — low-cost, successful missions (Mars, Moon)
- **Demographics**: 1.44B (largest, passed China 2023). 2,000+ ethnic groups, 1,600+ languages (Hindi, English official; 22 scheduled languages). Median age 28 (young — demographic dividend)
  - Hinduism (80%), Islam (14%), Christianity (2.3%), Sikhism (1.7%)
  - Caste system: officially abolished, deep social influence — discrimination against Dalits continues, affirmative action (reservations) in education/govt
- **Issues**: poverty (still 10-15% below poverty line), infrastructure gaps, pollution (Delhi worst air in world), caste discrimination, communal violence, regional separatism, bureaucracy/corruption

## European Union
- **Geography**: 27 member countries, 4.2M km² (7th). From Mediterranean to Arctic, Atlantic to Central Europe
  - Schengen Area (26 countries): no internal borders — free movement of people. Eurozone (20 countries): shared currency €
- **Government**: unique supranational entity — European Commission (executive), European Parliament (elected directly), European Council (member state leaders), Court of Justice of the EU
  - EU law supersedes national law in areas of competence. Single market: free movement of goods, services, capital, people
  - UK left 2020 (Brexit) — first (only) member to leave, complex ongoing relationship
- **Economy**: $18T GDP (collectively, 2nd/3rd largest). Euro is 2nd reserve currency
  - Strong in: automotive (Germany: VW, BMW, Mercedes), luxury goods (France: LVMH, Hermès), pharma, aerospace (Airbus), banking, tourism (most visited region globally)
  - Germany = largest EU economy (manufacturing export powerhouse), France (agriculture, luxury, military), Italy (fashion, tourism, manufacturing), Spain/Portugal/Greece (tourism, agriculture)
- **Demographics**: 450M (3rd after China/India). 24 official languages. Aging population (median 44). Migrant crisis (2015+, Middle East/Africa → Europe)
  - Largest cities: Paris (11M metro), London (9M metro, UK not EU), Berlin (6M), Madrid (6M), Rome (4M), Barcelona (5M), Vienna (2M), Prague (1.3M)
- **Challenges**: migration policy disagreements, EU-skepticism/populism, aging population, Ukraine war energy/defense response, competitiveness vs US/China

## Russia
- **Geography**: LARGEST country (17.1M km², 11% of world's land), 11 time zones. Spans Europe to Pacific
  - Siberia: vast, cold, sparsely populated, rich in natural resources (oil, gas, minerals, timber)
  - Lake Baikal: deepest (1,642m), holds 20% of world's fresh surface water
  - Ural Mountains: Europe/Asia boundary. Volga River: longest in Europe
- **Government**: federal semi-presidential republic (in practice, authoritarian). Vladimir Putin (president 2000-2008, 2012-present). United Russia party dominant. Heavy state control of media and politics
  - 2022: full-scale invasion of Ukraine — widely condemned, sanctions, war ongoing (2024+)
  - WWII (Great Patriotic War): 27M Soviet deaths (largest toll of any country in any war)
- **Economy**: 11th ($2T GDP, PPP = $5T). Highly dependent on oil/gas exports (40-50% of budget revenue, 30% of GDP)
  - Sanctions (2014 Crimea, 2022 Ukraine): significant economic pressure — shifted trade to China/India
- **Demographics**: 144M (9th). Declining (low birth rate, high adult male mortality, emigration). 190+ ethnic groups (Russian 80%, Tatar 4%, Ukrainian 1.5%)
  - Majority urban (Moscow 12M, St. Petersburg 5M). Russian Orthodox (majority), Muslim (10-15%)
- **International relations**: UN Security Council permanent member (veto power). Nuclear superpower (6,000+ warheads). Increasing tension with NATO, alignment with China
