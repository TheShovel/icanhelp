# Nutrition & Diet Basics

## Macronutrients
- **Protein**: 4 cal/g — fish, chicken, eggs, beans, tofu, dairy — repair/build tissue
- **Carbs**: 4 cal/g — rice, pasta, bread, fruits, vegetables — primary energy source
- **Fat**: 9 cal/g — oils, butter, nuts, avocado — hormone function, vitamin absorption
- **Fiber**: indigestible carb — oats, beans, broccoli, apples — digestion, satiety
- Daily recommendation: 45-65% carbs, 10-35% protein, 20-35% fat

## Vitamins & Minerals
- **Vitamin D**: sunlight, fatty fish, fortified milk — bone health, immune
- **Vitamin B12**: meat, fish, dairy, eggs — nerve function, red blood cells (vegans need supplement)
- **Iron**: red meat, spinach, lentils — oxygen transport (pair with vitamin C for absorption)
- **Calcium**: dairy, leafy greens, fortified plant milk — bones, teeth, muscle function
- **Vitamin C**: citrus, bell peppers, broccoli — immune, collagen, iron absorption
- **Potassium**: bananas, potatoes, avocados — blood pressure, muscle function
- **Magnesium**: nuts, seeds, dark chocolate, leafy greens — sleep, muscle relaxation, energy
- **Omega-3**: salmon, sardines, walnuts, flaxseed — brain health, anti-inflammatory

## Hydration
- Daily: ~2-3L water for women, ~3-4L for men (varies by activity/climate)
- Thirst is a late sign of dehydration — sip throughout day
- Caffeine and alcohol are mild diuretics (not dehydrating in moderation)
- Urine color: pale yellow = hydrated, dark = drink more
- Electrolytes lost in sweat: sodium, potassium, magnesium — replace if exercising heavily

## Common Diets
- **Mediterranean**: olive oil, fish, vegetables, whole grains, moderate wine — heart-healthy
- **Plant-based/vegan**: no animal products — need B12 supplement, watch iron/calcium/zinc
- **Keto**: very low carb (20-50g), high fat — forces ketosis, short-term weight loss
- **Intermittent fasting**: eating window (e.g. 16:8 — 16h fast, 8h eat) — calorie restriction method
- **DASH**: low sodium, high potassium/calcium/magnesium — designed for blood pressure

## Portion Guide (hand method)
- **Protein**: palm-sized (cooked) = ~20-30g protein
- **Vegetables**: fist-sized = ~1 cup
- **Carbs**: cupped hand = ~1/2 cup rice/pasta
- **Fats**: thumb-sized = ~1 tbsp oil/butter
- Plate method: 1/2 vegetables, 1/4 protein, 1/4 carbs

## Meal Prep
- Cook grains/beans in bulk, portion into containers
- Chop vegetables ahead, store in airtight containers
- Pre-portion snacks into bags
- Marinate protein the night before
- Freeze leftovers in single-serving portions
- Label everything with date

## Eating Out Tips
- Ask for dressing/sauce on the side
- Look for grilled/baked/steamed instead of fried
- Start with vegetable soup or salad to reduce hunger
- Share entrees or take half home (portion sizes are often 2x needed)
- Skip sugary drinks — water or unsweetened tea

## Common Myths
- **"Eggs are bad for cholesterol"** — dietary cholesterol has little effect on blood cholesterol for most people
- **"Carbs make you fat"** — excess calories make you fat, not carbs specifically
- **"Detox diets work"** — your liver and kidneys detox naturally
- **"Eating at night causes weight gain"** — total calories matter, not timing
- **"All fat is bad"** — unsaturated fats are essential and healthy
