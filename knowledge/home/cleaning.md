# Cleaning & Organization

## Room-by-Room Speed Clean (15 min)
- **Living room**: toss trash, fluff pillows, fold blankets, wipe surfaces, sweep visible floor
- **Kitchen**: load/start dishwasher, wipe counters and sink, sweep, take out trash
- **Bathroom**: spray shower + toilet with cleaner, wipe mirror, scrub toilet, wipe sink, mop
- **Bedroom**: make bed, put away clutter, quick vacuum visible floor
- **Pro tip**: clean top to bottom (dust settles), clean before vacuum (don't recirculate dust)

## Deep Cleaning Schedule
- **Weekly**: vacuum/mop floors, clean bathroom (toilet, shower, sink), change sheets, wipe kitchen surfaces
- **Monthly**: clean windows, dust blinds, wipe baseboards, clean under furniture, descale coffee maker
- **Quarterly**: wash curtains, clean inside fridge, vacuum under couch cushions, deep clean oven
- **Yearly**: wash walls, shampoo carpets, clean gutters, power wash exterior, clean dryer vent

## Kitchen Cleaning
- **Oven**: baking soda + water paste, spread inside, let sit overnight, spray vinegar, wipe — avoid chemical oven cleaners (toxic fumes)
- **Microwave**: bowl of water + lemon slices, microwave 3 min, steam loosens grime, wipe clean
- **Fridge**: remove all, toss expired, wipe shelves with baking soda water, put back organized
- **Dishwasher**: run empty with vinegar in top rack (remove mineral deposits), then baking soda (deodorize)
- **Cutting boards**: rub with lemon + salt, let sit, rinse. Bleach solution for raw meat
- **Cast iron**: never soap (removes seasoning). Scrub with salt + oil, rinse, dry on stove, light oil coat

## Bathroom Cleaning
- **Shower mold**: bleach spray + let sit 10 min, scrub grout with baking soda paste + old toothbrush
- **Hard water stains**: vinegar soak (paper towels soaked, stuck to glass), scrub with baking soda
- **Toilet**: pour cleaner in bowl, let sit, scrub under rim with brush, flush. Clean outside with spray
- **Showerhead mineral buildup**: fill bag with vinegar, tie around head, soak overnight, run water after

## Stain Removal (Laundry)
- **Coffee/tea**: cold water rinse + dish soap + white vinegar. Old stains: rub with laundry detergent, soak
- **Red wine**: blot salt on fresh stain (absorbs), then cold water + dish soap. Dried: hydrogen peroxide + dish soap
- **Grease/oil**: dish soap directly on stain, rub in, wash hot (if fabric allows). Cornstarch/baking soda absorbs first
- **Blood**: cold water only (hot sets protein stain). Hydrogen peroxide + dish soap work
- **Grass**: hydrogen peroxide or white vinegar, rub, wash. Pre-treat with laundry enzyme cleaner
- **Ink**: rubbing alcohol (place paper towel behind stain, dab with alcohol, keep moving to clean towel area)
- **Sweat stains**: baking soda + hydrogen peroxide + water paste, rub, let sit 30 min, wash
- **General**: treat ASAP, don't put stained item in dryer (heat sets stain), test on hidden area first

## Organization Systems
- **Keep/discard**: don't keep "just in case" items you haven't used in 2 years
- **One in, one out**: for every new item, donate/ discard one old item
- **Vertical storage**: use shelf risers, hanging organizers, drawer dividers — stack less, see everything
- **Visible storage**: clear containers, open shelves, pegboards — you can't use what you can't see
- **Label everything**: label maker or washi tape + sharpie — helps everyone in household maintain
- **Entry way**: hooks + shoe rack + tray for keys/wallet — catch-all before they enter the house
- **Command center**: calendar, to-do list, mail basket, pens — in kitchen or hallway

## Decluttering Method (KonMari)
1. **Tidy by category, not location**: clothes → books → papers → misc → sentimental
2. **Keep only what sparks joy**: physically hold each item, decide if it brings joy
3. **Thank items before discarding**: ritual for letting go (reduce guilt)
4. **Vertical folding**: fold clothes to stand upright (drawer), not stack — see everything at once
5. **Storage**: everything has a home, return after use, less is more
- Alternative: **Minimalism**: reduce to essentials (less stuff = less to clean, organize, maintain)
- **Swedish death cleaning**: declutter gradually (don't leave burden for family)

## Home Efficiency Tips
- **Entry mat**: keep dirt out (outside + inside door), saves cleaning
- **No shoes indoors**: drastically reduces dirt, dust, and cleaning time (offer slippers for guests)
- **Make bed every morning**: instantly makes room look clean, takes <2 min
- **Clean as you cook**: wash dishes while food simmers — no post-meal mountain
- **Nightly reset**: 10 min before bed — pick up living room, clear kitchen, set coffee — wake up to clean house
- **One-touch rule**: handle something once — don't put mail on counter to deal with later, deal now

## Cleaning Products
- **All-purpose**: diluted dish soap (safe, cheap, works on most surfaces)
- **Disinfectant**: bleach solution (1 tbsp per gallon water, let sit 10 min) or 70%+ alcohol
- **Glass cleaner**: 1 part vinegar + 3 parts water + drop of dish soap in spray bottle
- **Wood furniture**: mix 1/4 cup olive oil + 1/4 cup vinegar + 10 drops lemon essential oil — dust spray
- **Grout cleaner**: baking soda paste + hydrogen peroxide — scrub, let sit, rinse
- **Air freshener**: simmer citrus peels + cinnamon + cloves on stove (avoids artificial sprays)
- **Abrasive cleaner**: baking soda + water paste — gentle scouring for sinks, tubs
- Never mix: bleach + ammonia (toxic gas), bleach + vinegar (chlorine gas), hydrogen peroxide + vinegar (peracetic acid)
