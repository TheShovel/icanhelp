# Home Appliance Care & Troubleshooting

## Refrigerator
### Common Issues
- **Not cooling**: check condenser coils (clean if dusty), verify condenser fan runs, check compressor start relay
- **Water leaking**: clogged defrost drain (clear with hot water + turkey baster), leveling (tilt back slightly)
- **Ice maker not working**: check water line (frozen?), replace inlet valve, test ice maker module
- **Running too much**: dirty coils, door seal leak (dollar bill test), thermostat failing, overfilled
- **Strange noises**: evaporator fan (ice buildup), condenser fan (blade obstruction), compressor (bad mount)

### Maintenance
- Clean condenser coils every 6 months (coil brush + vacuum)
- Check door seals — should hold a piece of paper when door is closed
- Keep coils 2-3 inches from wall for airflow
- Set temp: 37-40°F (fridge), 0°F (freezer)
- Replace water filter every 6 months

## Washing Machine
### Common Issues
- **Won't spin**: unbalanced load (redistribute), lid switch broken, drive belt snapped, drain pump clogged
- **Not draining**: check drain hose for kink, clean pump filter, clear debris from pump impeller
- **Leaking**: check fill hoses (replace braided steel every 5 years), door boot (front-load), drain pump seal
- **Shaking/vibrating**: level feet (adjust + lock), shipping bolts removed? (new machines), floor uneven
- **Foul smell**: front-load boot mold (run tub clean cycle with vinegar or Affresh), leave door open after use
- **Error codes**: look up manual online — most common: drain issue, lid lock failure, water inlet

### Maintenance
- Clean dispensers monthly (remove, soak in hot vinegar)
- Run tub clean cycle monthly (HE washer)
- Leave door open between uses (front-load)
- Check hoses annually — replace if bulging, cracked, or corroded
- Level the machine (putty knife under feet, tighten lock nuts)

## Dryer
### Common Issues
- **Not heating**: thermal fuse blown (most common), heating element failed, gas igniter bad, thermostat
- **Not tumbling**: belt broken (replace), drum rollers seized, motor thermal overload, idler pulley
- **Takes too long**: clogged vent (clean lint from exhaust duct — fire hazard!), overloaded, lint screen full
- **Loud noises**: drum support roller bad, belt glazed, blower wheel loose
- **Error codes**: check for moisture sensor bars (clean with fine sandpaper)

### Maintenance
- Clean lint filter after every load
- Clean dryer vent duct annually (lint brush + vacuum from outside vent)
- Check vent flap opens when running
- Keep area around dryer clear (lint is highly flammable)
- Clean moisture sensor bars with fine steel wool every 6 months

## Dishwasher
### Common Issues
- **Not cleaning**: dirty spray arms (remove and clean holes with toothpick), clogged drain filter, water temp too low (use booster heater), wrong detergent
- **Not draining**: food jam in drain impeller, garbage disposal plug not removed, drain hose kinked
- **Leaking**: door gasket worn (replace), over-sudsing (use only dishwasher detergent), hose connections loose
- **Dishes not drying**: rinse aid empty, heating element failed (air dry enabled?), plastic items (don't dry well)
- **Stuck on wash cycle**: float switch stuck (food under float), inlet valve failing

### Maintenance
- Scrape but don't rinse dishes (detergent needs food particles to work)
- Run hot water at sink before starting (ensures immediate hot fill)
- Clean filter monthly (bottom of tub — twist and rinse)
- Run vinegar cycle (cup on top rack) + baking soda (bottom) every 3 months
- Use rinse aid for spot-free drying

## Oven & Stove
### Electric Stove
- **Element not heating**: bad element (visible burn/open), bad infinite switch (replace with same rating)
- **Element stays on**: stuck relay on control board (replace board) or bad switch

### Gas Stove
- **Burner won't light**: clogged burner cap (clean ports with paperclip), igniter dirty, igniter module bad
- **Yellow / lazy flame**: needs air adjustment (turn shutter screw), clogged orifice (spider webs!)
- **Gas smell**: immediate — turn off, don't use electronics, call gas company
- **Clicking constantly**: wet igniter (dry it), dirty switch

### Oven
- **Not heating**: bake element open (use multimeter — 40 ohms typically), temperature sensor bad, control board
- **Temp inaccurate**: calibrate (±30°F adjustment in settings), replace temp sensor, check door gasket
- **Door won't close**: hinge broken (replace), door gasket loose

### Self-Cleaning Cycle
- Remove racks (they discolor at high heat)
- Wipe up large spills first
- Run when nobody is home (smoke/lock)
- Some ovens lock during cycle (normal)
- Alternative: steam clean (newer models, less heat)

## Garbage Disposal
- **Humming but not spinning**: jammed — cut power, use hex wrench in center hole (bottom of unit) to rotate manually
- **Not running at all**: press reset button on bottom, check wall switch, overload protector tripped
- **Leaking**: replace sink flange gasket, tighten bolts, check dishwasher hose connection
- **Smelling**: grind lemon peels + ice cubes, run cold water while grinding
- Don't put: bones, fibrous veggies (celery, corn husks), coffee grounds (in bulk), grease/oil, pasta/rice (expands), potato peels (starch paste)
- Run cold water 15-30 sec after grinding to flush debris

## Water Heater
### Tank (Storage)
- **No hot water**: pilot light out (gas — relight), heating element failed (electric), tripped breaker
- **Not enough hot water**: thermostat too low (set to 120-140°F), sediment buildup (drain/flush annually), dip tube broken, undersized tank
- **Leaking**: T&P valve (drain or replace), tank corrosion (replace the whole tank), loose drain valve
- **Rumbling**: sediment buildup — drain and flush tank (connect garden hose, open drain + T&P valve)
- **Rusty water**: anode rod depleted (replace annually extend tank life), corroded tank (replace)

### Tankless (On-Demand)
- **No hot water**: gas valve shut, flow too low (minimum GPM not met), vent blocked, freeze protection active
- **Water too hot**: over temp setting, flow too low (turn water on faster or adjust temp)
- **Temperature fluctuates**: other fixtures in use, gas pressure issues, heat exchanger scaled
- **Maintenance**: descale annually (vinegar or citric acid through service valves), clean inlet filter

## Small Appliances
- **Microwave not heating**: door switches (most common — microswitches fail), high voltage diode bad, magnetron failed
- **Toaster not staying down**: latch mechanism broken (replace), solenoid weak
- **Vacuum cleaner loses suction**: full bag/dustbin, clogged filter (wash/change), brush roll jammed (hair), hose blockage
- **Slow cooker not heating**: thermal fuse blown, control board failed

## When to Repair vs Replace
- Repairs costing > 50% of new price → replace
- Age: fridge 10-15y, washer/dryer 8-12y, dishwasher 8-10y, water heater 8-12y, microwave 8-10y
- Major component failures (compressor, main control board) often justify replacement
- Energy efficiency: new appliances use 20-50% less energy than 10+ yr old models
- Parts availability: if parts are discontinued, replace

## General Safety
- Unplug before working on any appliance
- Use a multimeter to test continuity, voltage, resistance
- For gas appliances: shut off gas line at valve before service
- Test smoke/CO detectors monthly (combination recommended near fuel-burning appliances)
- Know location of: circuit breaker panel, main water shutoff, gas shutoff
- Electrical: if unsure, call a licensed electrician
- Gas: call utility company or licensed plumber/HVAC tech
