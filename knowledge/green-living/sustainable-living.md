# Sustainable Living & Green Practices

## Overview
Green living (sustainable living) aims to reduce your environmental footprint through conscious choices in consumption, energy, waste, transportation, and lifestyle.

## Energy Efficiency at Home

### Quick Wins (Low Cost, High Impact)
- **LED bulbs**: Use 75% less energy, last 25x longer
- **Smart power strips**: Eliminate phantom loads (5-10% of bill)
- **Programmable thermostat**: Save 10-15% on heating/cooling
- **Weatherstripping/caulking**: Seal air leaks (~$20, saves 10-20%)
- **Low-flow fixtures**: Showerheads (1.5 GPM), faucet aerators (0.5 GPM)
- **Cold water laundry**: 90% of energy goes to heating water

### Medium Investment
- **Insulation upgrade**: Attic (R-38+), walls, basement rim joists
- **Heat pump HVAC**: 300-400% efficient vs gas furnace (95%)
- **Heat pump water heater**: 2-3x more efficient than electric resistance
- **Induction cooktop**: 85% efficient vs gas (40%) or electric (75%)
- **Energy Star appliances**: 10-50% less energy

### Major Investment
- **Solar PV**: 5-10 year payback, 25+ year lifespan
- **Battery storage**: Resilience + time-of-use arbitrage
- **Geothermal heat pump**: Most efficient heating/cooling
- **Home energy audit**: Blower door test, thermal imaging

### Behavioral Changes
- **Setback thermostat**: 68°F winter day, 60°F night/away; 78°F summer
- **Zone heating/cooling**: Only condition occupied rooms
- **Line dry clothes**: Dryer = 6% of home energy
- **Unplug chargers**: Phantom load adds up
- **Use microwave/toaster oven**: 50-80% less energy than oven

## Renewable Energy

### Solar PV Basics
```
System sizing: Annual kWh / (peak sun hours × 365 × 0.75 derating)
Example: 10,000 kWh/yr ÷ (4.5 × 365 × 0.75) = 8.1 kW system

Cost: $2.50-3.50/W installed (before incentives)
Federal tax credit: 30% (through 2032)
Net metering: Varies by utility/state
```

### Community Solar
- No roof required
- Subscribe to portion of solar farm
- 10-15% savings on electric bill
- Portable if you move

### Green Power Programs
- Utility green pricing: Pay premium for renewable energy
- RECs (Renewable Energy Certificates): Buy separately
- Community Choice Aggregation: City negotiates cleaner power

### Reduce Driving
- **Combine trips**: Cold starts = 2x emissions
- **Carpool/vanpool**: 2-4x occupancy efficiency
- **Public transit**: 76% less CO₂ per passenger mile
- **Bike/walk**: Zero emissions, health benefits
- **Remote work**: 1 day/week = 20% commute reduction
- **E-bike for <10 mi trips**: 400-1000 MPGe equivalent

### EV Charging
- **Level 1 (120V)**: 3-5 mi/hr, no install needed
- **Level 2 (240V)**: 20-30 mi/hr, $500-1500 install
- **DC Fast Charge**: 150-300 mi/hr, for road trips
- **Charge off-peak**: Lower rates, cleaner grid
- **Solar + EV**: Charge from your roof

## Waste Reduction

### Waste Hierarchy (Most to Least Preferred)
```
1. REFUSE - Don't accept what you don't need
2. REDUCE - Buy less, choose quality over quantity
3. REUSE - Repair, repurpose, buy secondhand
4. RECYCLE - Properly sort, clean, recycle
5. ROT - Compost organics
6. LANDFILL - Last resort
```

### Single-Use Plastic Alternatives

|------|-------------|

| Ziploc bags | Silicone bags, glass containers |

### Food Waste (30-40% of US food wasted)
- **Meal plan**: Shop with list, buy only needed
- **Proper storage**: Extend produce life
- **Understand dates**: "Best by" ≠ unsafe
- **Freeze before spoiling**: Bread, fruit, herbs, leftovers
- **Compost**: Backyard, vermicompost, municipal, countertop (Lomi, etc.)
- **Eat leftovers**: Designate "leftover night"

### Recycling Right
```
ALWAYS recycle (clean & dry):
- Aluminum/steel cans
- Paper/cardboard (flattened)
- Glass bottles/jars
- Plastic #1 (PET), #2 (HDPE), #5 (PP)

NEVER in curbside:
- Plastic bags/film (store drop-off)
- Styrofoam
- Food-soiled containers
- Tanglers (hoses, wires, chains)
- Hazardous waste (batteries, electronics, chemicals)

CHECK LOCAL RULES - Varies significantly
```

## Water Conservation

### Indoor
- **Toilets**: Dual-flush (0.8/1.6 GPF) or displacement device
- **Showers**: 5-min timer, Navy shower (off while lathering)
- **Faucets**: Aerators (0.5 GPM bathroom, 1.5 GPM kitchen)
- **Leaks**: 1 drip/sec = 3,000 gal/yr; fix immediately
- **Appliances**: Full loads only, Energy Star dishwasher/washer

### Outdoor
- **Native/drought-tolerant plants**: 50-75% less water
- **Mulch 2-3"**: Reduces evaporation 70%
- **Drip irrigation**: 90% efficient vs sprinkler (50-70%)
- **Rain barrels**: 55 gal × 1" rain × 1000 sq ft roof = 600 gal
- **Greywater**: Laundry-to-landscape (check codes)
- **Water timing**: Early morning (4-8 AM), less evaporation

### Sustainable Eating
- **Plant-forward**: Meat as condiment, not centerpiece
- **Local/seasonal**: Lower transport, supports local farms
- **Regenerative agriculture**: Builds soil carbon
- **Less processed**: Whole foods = less packaging, energy
- **Sustainable seafood**: MSC/ASC certified, lower trophic level

### Home Growing
- **Herbs on windowsill**: High value, low space
- **Container gardening**: Tomatoes, peppers, lettuce
- **Raised beds**: Better soil, drainage, yield
- **Perennials**: Asparagus, rhubarb, berries, fruit trees
- **Compost**: Close the nutrient loop

## Conscious Consumption

### Clothing (Fashion = 10% global emissions)
- **Buy less, choose well**: Cost per wear metric
- **Natural fibers**: Cotton, linen, wool, hemp (biodegradable)
- **Avoid polyester**: Microplastics, fossil-based
- **Secondhand**: Thrift, consignment, Poshmark, ThredUp
- **Repair**: Sew buttons, patch holes, resole shoes
- **Capsule wardrobe**: 30-40 versatile pieces

### Electronics
- **Repair first**: iFixit, local repair cafés
- **Refurbished**: Apple Certified, Back Market, eBay
- **E-waste recycling**: Certified e-Stewards/R2 recyclers
- **Right to Repair**: Support legislation
- **Extend life**: Battery replacement, OS updates

### Household Products
- **Concentrated/refillable**: Cleaners, soaps, detergents
- **Plastic-free**: Bar soap, shampoo bars, toothpaste tabs
- **DIY cleaners**: Vinegar, baking soda, castile soap
- **Reusable**: Cloth napkins, towels, mop pads, dryer balls

## Green Finance

### Banking
- **Divest from fossil fuels**: Check bank fossil fuel financing
- **Green banks**: Aspiration, Amalgamated, Clean Energy Credit Union
- **Credit unions**: Community-focused, often greener

### Investing
- **ESG funds**: Environmental, Social, Governance screens
- **Fossil-free funds**: No oil/gas/coal (e.g., SPYX, ETHO)
- **Green bonds**: Fund climate projects
- **Community investing**: CDFIs, microfinance
- **Shareholder advocacy**: Vote proxies, file resolutions

### Carbon Offsets (Last Resort)
```
Quality criteria:
- Additionality: Wouldn't happen without funding
- Permanence: 100+ year storage
- Verification: Gold Standard, Verra, ACR
- No leakage: Doesn't shift problem elsewhere

Better: Direct air capture (Climeworks), verified reforestation
Avoid: Avoided deforestation (leakage risk), renewable credits (often not additional)
```

- **Annual CO₂e**: Target <2 tCO₂e/person (global avg 4.5, US 16)
- **Home energy**: kWh electricity, therms gas
- **Transportation**: VMT, flights, fuel
- **Waste**: Landfill volume, diversion rate
- **Water**: Gallons per capita per day
