# Physics Basics

## Classical Mechanics
- F = ma — Force = mass × acceleration
- v = d/t — velocity = distance / time
- a = Δv/t — acceleration = change in velocity / time
- KE = ½mv² — kinetic energy
- PE = mgh — gravitational potential energy
- W = Fd — work = force × distance
- P = W/t — power = work / time
- Momentum p = mv — conservation of momentum (total before = total after)
- Newton's 1st: object in motion stays in motion unless acted on by force (inertia)
- Newton's 2nd: F = ma
- Newton's 3rd: every action has equal opposite reaction
- Gravity: g = 9.8 m/s² on Earth (varies slightly by location)
- Friction: F = μN (coefficient × normal force) — static > kinetic
- Terminal velocity: when air resistance = weight, object stops accelerating

## Thermodynamics
- **1st Law**: energy cannot be created/destroyed, only converted (ΔU = Q - W)
- **2nd Law**: entropy always increases — heat flows hot → cold, cannot be 100% efficient
- **3rd Law**: absolute zero (-273.15°C) is unattainable
- Temperature scales: F = 1.8C + 32, K = C + 273.15
- Specific heat: Q = mcΔT — energy to change temperature
- Latent heat: Q = mL — energy to change phase (solid↔liquid↔gas) without temp change
- Conduction: heat transfer through direct contact (metal good conductor)
- Convection: heat transfer through fluid movement (hot air rises)
- Radiation: heat transfer through electromagnetic waves (sun, fire)

## Waves
- v = fλ — wave speed = frequency × wavelength
- Sound speed: ~343 m/s in air (at 20°C), faster in water, fastest in solids
- Light speed: 3×10⁸ m/s in vacuum (slowest in denser media)
- Doppler effect: source moving toward you → higher frequency (blueshift), away → lower (redshift)
- Refraction: light bends entering different medium (snell's law: n₁sinθ₁ = n₂sinθ₂)
- Diffraction: waves bend around obstacles (more when wavelength > obstacle size)
- Interference: waves combine — constructive (same phase = bigger), destructive (opposite = cancel)
- Resonance: system driven at natural frequency → amplitude grows (pushing a swing)
- Electromagnetic spectrum: radio → microwave → infrared → visible → UV → X-ray → gamma

## Electricity
- V = IR — voltage = current × resistance (Ohm's law)
- P = IV — power = current × voltage
- Series: Rₜ = R₁+R₂+R₃..., same current, voltage divides
- Parallel: 1/Rₜ = 1/R₁+1/R₂+..., same voltage, current divides
- Capacitors: store energy in electric field (C = Q/V)
- Inductors: store energy in magnetic field, resist current change
- AC (wall outlet): 120V/60Hz (US), 230V/50Hz (most other countries)
- Household: breakers trip at 15-20A per circuit, GFCI for wet areas
- Danger: >10mA painful, >100mA can be lethal (tetanize + stop heart)

## Light & Optics
- Reflection: angle of incidence = angle of reflection
- Mirrors: plane (same size), concave (magnify, focus), convex (shrink, wider view)
- Lenses: convex (converge, magnify, farsighted correction), concave (diverge, shrink, nearsighted)
- Lens equation: 1/f = 1/d₀ + 1/dᵢ
- Human eye: pupil (aperture), lens (focus), retina (sensor), 120M rods (brightness) + 6M cones (color)
- Color: red (~700nm), green (~550nm), blue (~470nm) — RGB primary, eye most sensitive to green
- Lasers: coherent (same phase), collimated (parallel), monochromatic (one wavelength)

## Modern Physics
- E = mc² — energy = mass × speed of light² (nuclear reactions, tiny mass = huge energy)
- Quantum mechanics: energy is quantized, particles are also waves, observers affect measurement
- Heisenberg uncertainty: can't know both position and momentum exactly (Δx·Δp ≥ h/4π)
- Photoelectric effect: light knocks electrons from metal (Einstein, proved light is particles/photons)
- Wave-particle duality: light and electrons act as both waves and particles depending on measurement
- Special relativity: nothing faster than light, time slows at high speed, length contracts
- General relativity: gravity bends spacetime, affects time (GPS satellites need correction)
- Standard model: quarks (up/down/charm/strange/top/bottom), leptons (electron, muon, tau, neutrinos), bosons (photon, gluon, W/Z, Higgs)
