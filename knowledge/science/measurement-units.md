# Measurement Units, Constants & Conversions

## SI Base Units
- Length: meter (m)
- Mass: kilogram (kg)
- Time: second (s)
- Electric current: ampere (A)
- Temperature: kelvin (K)
- Amount: mole (mol)
- Luminous intensity: candela (cd)

## SI Prefixes
- `p` (pico) 1e-12, `n` (nano) 1e-9, `µ` (micro) 1e-6, `m` (milli) 1e-3
- `c` (centi) 1e-2, `k` (kilo) 1e3, `M` (mega) 1e6, `G` (giga) 1e9, `T` (tera) 1e12

## Common Conversions
- 1 inch = 2.54 cm (exact); 1 foot = 0.3048 m; 1 mile = 1.609 km
- 1 pound (lb) = 0.453592 kg; 1 kg = 2.20462 lb
- 1 gallon (US) = 3.785 L; 1 liter = 0.264172 US gal
- 1 ounce (oz) = 28.3495 g; 1 fluid oz (US) = 29.5735 mL
- °C to °F: `F = C * 9/5 + 32`; °F to °C: `C = (F - 32) * 5/9`
- K to °C: `C = K - 273.15`
- 1 calorie = 4.184 J; 1 kWh = 3.6e6 J; 1 BTU = 1055 J
- 1 horsepower = 745.7 W; 1 atm = 101325 Pa = 760 mmHg

## Physical Constants
- Speed of light c = 299792458 m/s
- Gravitational constant G = 6.67430e-11 m³ kg⁻¹ s⁻²
- Planck constant h = 6.62607015e-34 J·s
- Elementary charge e = 1.602176634e-19 C
- Avogadro N_A = 6.02214076e23 mol⁻¹
- Boltzmann k_B = 1.380649e-23 J/K
- Electron mass = 9.1093837e-31 kg; Proton mass = 1.6726219e-27 kg
- Standard gravity g = 9.80665 m/s²
- Atmospheric pressure = 101.325 kPa

## Data Sizes
- 1 KiB = 1024 bytes; 1 MiB = 1024 KiB; 1 GiB = 1024 MiB
- 1 KB (decimal) = 1000 bytes (storage marketing)
- 8 bits = 1 byte; 1 nibble = 4 bits

## Time
- 1 minute = 60 s; 1 hour = 3600 s; 1 day = 86400 s
- 1 year (Julian) = 365.25 days; 1 leap year = 366 days
- Unix epoch = 1970-01-01 00:00:00 UTC
