# Plant-Based Cooking: Proteins & Substitutions

## Tofu Mastery
- **Extra firm**: press 15-30 min (wrap in towel, put heavy pan on top) to remove excess water. Marinade: soy sauce + garlic + ginger + sesame oil. Cooking: pan-fry (4-5 min each side, golden crisp), bake (400°F 20-30 min, flip halfway), grill, air fry (375°F 12-15 min). Use: stir-fry, tacos, sandwiches, salads
- **Soft/silken tofu**: cream sauces (blend: silken tofu + nutritional yeast + lemon + garlic + salt = alfredo). Smoothies (protein boost — adds creaminess). Scramble (crumble, cook with onion + garlic + turmeric + nutritional yeast + black salt = eggy flavor). Cheesecake (blend + cashews + lemon + sugar/agave + vanilla = baked cheesecake)
- **Baking**: tofu can soak up flavors better after freezing → thaw → press (texture becomes more porous + spongy, soaks up more marinade). Pulled tofu: freeze, thaw, press, shred, season + bake

## Tempeh Mastery
- **Steam first**: 10 min (removes bitterness, pre-cooks). Marinade for 30 min+. Cooking methods: pan-fry (sliced thin, 3-4 min per side), bake (400°F 20 min, baste halfway), crumble (as ground meat in chili, tacos, bolognese). Smoky maple bacon: slice thin, marinade (soy sauce + maple syrup + smoked paprika + liquid smoke), bake at 375°F 15 min each side
- **Fermentation**: once opened, tempeh can be frozen (lasts months). At room temperature: only a couple days, test for ammonia smell before using

## Seitan Mastery
- **Vital wheat gluten** base: 1 cup VWG + 2 tbsp nutritional yeast + 1 tsp spices (garlic, onion, paprika). Mix with 3/4 cup broth + 2 tbsp soy sauce. Knead 5 min. Steam (30 min), simmer in broth (30-45 min), bake (350°F 30 min), or fry immediately
  - Chicken-style: add chickpea flour + lemon + herbs + celery seed. Beef-style: add mushroom powder + beet powder + soy sauce + smoked paprika + porcini powder. Italian sausage: fennel seed + red pepper flake + garlic + oregano + paprika
  - Texture variations: low kneading = tender, more = chewier. Longer simmer = firmer. Add white beans (mash and blend = tenderer texture) or chickpeas

## Legume Mastery
- **Lentils**: brown (hold shape, earthy — salads, soups). Red (split, melt into mush — dal, thick stews, soups). French/Puy (firm, peppery — best for cold salads). Black beluga (caviar-like, firm, elegant). Cooking: brown 25-30 min, red 12-15 min, French 20-25 min. Don't salt until end (salt slows softening)
  - Lentil bolognese: brown onions + garlic, add lentils (1 cup dry) + veg broth (2 cups) + crushed tomatoes (28oz) + herbs. Simmer 30-40 min. Use over pasta, top with nutritional yeast. Lentil meatballs: mash cooked lentils + breadcrumbs + onion + garlic + herbs, bake 375°F 20 min
- **Chickpeas**: roast (toss in oil + spices, 400°F 30 min = crunchy snack). Blend (hummus: chickpeas + tahini + lemon + garlic + olive oil. Secret: use warm chickpeas, process for 5 min = smoothest hummus. Aquafaba: can liquid → whip like egg whites (3 tbsp = 1 egg white), meringue, mousse). Chickpea frittata: blend chickpea flour + water + onion + veg + chickpeas, pan-fry until set

## Mushroom Mastery
- **Meaty mushrooms**: king oyster (scallops: slice thick, score, marinate, sear). Shiitake (bacon: thin slices, marinade soy + maple + smoked paprika, bake crispy). Lion's mane (crab cakes: shred, sauté, bind with mayo/breadcrumbs). Portobello (steak: marinate + grill, or burger: roasted portobello cap). Maitake (pulled pork: shred, season, roast)
  - Dried mushrooms: rehydrate in warm water (30 min). Reserve liquid = broth/stock (add to soups, stews, risotto). Powder dried shiitake in spice grinder = umami powder (add to anything for savory depth)

## Nuts & Seeds Mastery
- **Cashew cream**: soak raw cashews 4 hr (or boil 10 min). Drain, blend with water (1:1 ratio for cream, 1:3 for milk). Base for: sauces, dressings, soups, desserts, cheese, sour cream. Season: nutritional yeast + lemon + garlic = "cheese" sauce. No soaking: with high-speed blender (Vitamix, Blendtec), you can skip soaking — blend with hot water for 2-3 min
- **Walnut meat**: pulse walnuts + soy sauce + smoked paprika + maple syrup + garlic in food processor until crumbly. Use: tacos, pasta, salads, stuffed peppers. No-cook "taco meat"
- **Hemp hearts**: sprinkle on anything (oatmeal, salads, toast, smoothies, roasted vegetables). High protein (10g/3 tbsp), omega-3. No need to grind
- **Chia seeds**: egg replacement (1 tbsp + 3 tbsp water, sit 15 min = gel = 1 egg). Pudding (3 tbsp chia + 1 cup plant milk, stir, fridge overnight). Thickener for jam, smoothie, pudding. Blend for creamy texture

## Plant Milks
- **Oat milk**: best for coffee (creamy, neutral, doesn't curdle). Highest in sugar (digested quickly). Barista edition: extra creamy (higher fat + stabilizers). Not high protein. One ingredient: oats + water — blend, strain through nut milk bag/fine towel. Use in lattes
- **Soy milk**: highest protein (7g/cup, closest to dairy milk). Best for baking + savory recipes. Good frothing (for lattes). Get unsweetened (avoid added sugar)
- **Almond milk**: low calorie, low protein. Thin, best for cereal. Good for low-carb diet. Can't froth well (watery, doesn't hold structure). DIY: soak almonds 8 hr, blend with water 1:3 ratio, strain
- **Coconut milk**: canned (full fat for curries, soups, desserts). Carton (for cereal, coffee). High in saturated fat. Cream (from can top) = whipped dessert (chill can, scoop solid top, whip with sugar). Great for curries + creamy soups
